About

Why? Just why?

This application was developed to try and help me do better in a formula 1 fantasy league. To put it nicely I was quite horrible so I wanted to find out what combination of drivers was 'theoretically' the best. I therefore made this application to help me do that.

How it works

The application works by creating a huge database of all possible combinations of drivers and constructors. It then orders this database based on the highest average points of the team. Now this is all good and well, but we do have cost limitations, therefore the application only returns the highest average scoring teams in your budget. The application also allows you to select exactly what drivers and constructors you want to analyse. The default includes all drivers and constructors.

Important assumptions!!

The list takes streaks into account, and ASSUMES that the streak points will be achieved. For example if one driver is on a streak this week, those points will be added to the streak points and also to the total predicted points. There is however a high possibility that that driver will not get their streak. You can choose to exclude the streak points in the predicted points by unchecking the streak points box before performing the search.

Disclaimer

Although this application provides insight into the highest average scoring teams in your budget, it does NOT mean that those teams will be the best on race day. As we know, anything can happen with crashes, penalties and mechanical problems. This application should therefore not be seen as an application that will provide the best teams on race day, but rather an application that can help you select better teams.

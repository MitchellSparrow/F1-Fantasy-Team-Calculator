About

Why? Just why?

This application was developed to try and help me do better in a formula 1 fantasy league. To put it nicely I was quite horrible so I wanted to find out what combination of drivers was 'theoretically' the best. I therefore made this application to help me do that. After using it for a few races I found that it did help me with my team choice quite a bit and so I thought that others could possibly benefit from it too.

What the future holds

I hope to add quite a bit of functionality in the future. I am a huge fan of Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning, and I would love to someday implement some sort of prediction model into this application. Some other elements that I hope to add in future would be a risk factor based on the number of times each driver has crashed, as well as some sort of historic data based on weather conditions and different tracks.

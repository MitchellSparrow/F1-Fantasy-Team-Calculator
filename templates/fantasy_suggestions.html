Fantasy Formula One Team Suggestions

The following page provides you with a free tool that suggests teams to choose from for the next grand prix. This works by creating a large database of all possible combinations of teams, before ordering them from highest scoring (average points for the season so far) to lowest scoring. The top 30 teams in your budget are then listed in the table.

You also have the choice to ASSUME that the drivers and constructors who are on streaks for the next grand prix WILL achieve these points. This is obviously an assumption however streak points are very important in fantasy and can mean the difference between winning and loosing. You also have the choice to exclude specific drivers and constructors from the list, if you don't like them, you can't sub them in, or you think that they just look funny.

For a more detailed explanation on how this works please click the button below which will take you to our user manual page. There are also explanations of our other tools such as our betting data analysis tool which can be found here.
